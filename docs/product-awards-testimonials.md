## Forzudo Rojo

### Awards and Scores

- **Concours Mondial de Bruxelles / Spirit Selection 2017: Double Gold / Grand Gold.**
  - Evidence: Guia Gourmet de Leon says Forzudo Rojo received the 2017 Double Gold, the top
    distinction, at Concours Mondial de Bruxelles, and La Despensa del Bierzo describes it as the
    Grand Gold Medal winner at the Brussels world competition.
  - Sources:
    - https://www.guiagourmetdeleon.es/vermouth-forzudo/
    - https://ladespensadelbierzo.es/producto/vermouth-forzudo-rojo/
    - https://www.infobierzo.com/tendencias/un-vermouth-del-bierzo-forzudo-y-campeon-del-mundo-en-bruselas_578439_102.html

- **IWSC 2018: Silver Outstanding / Plata Excelente.**
  - Evidence: Forzudo's own article says Forzudo Rojo achieved the highest Spanish vermouth
    distinction in IWSC 2018; IWSC's press coverage index also lists the Forzudo article with the
    same claim.
  - Sources:
    - https://forzudo.es/vermouth-forzudo-rojo-plata-excelente-en-el-iwsc-2018/
    - https://iwsc.net/coverage/index/23

- **Guia Penin 2019: 92 points, "excellent" category.**
  - Evidence: Infobierzo reports that Guia Penin 2019 awarded Forzudo Rojo 92 points and explains
    that 90-94 points maps to "excellent" / very high quality.
  - Sources:
    - https://www.infobierzo.com/tendencias/la-guia-penin-reconoce-la-excelencia-de-los-vermuts-forzudos-del-bierzo_452575_102.html
    - https://forzudo.es/la-guia-penin-reconoce-la-excelencia-de-los-vermuts-de-el-bierzo/

- **London Spirits Competition 2022: Bronze Medal, 70 points.**
  - Evidence: The official 2022 London Spirits Competition results list Vermouth Forzudo Rojo by
    Forzudo Food & Drinks SL with Bronze Medal and 70 points.
  - Source: https://londonspiritscompetition.com/en/competition-global-results/2022/

### Ratings and Testimonials

- **Vivino: 3.9-4.0 rating from about 50 total ratings.**
  - Evidence: Vivino pages list Forzudo Vermouth Rojo at 4.0 in the US page and 3.9 in the Spanish
    page, with the Forzudo winery shown around 50 total ratings.
  - Sources:
    - https://www.vivino.com/US/en/forzudo-vermouth-rojo/w/8062529
    - https://www.vivino.com/es/forzudo-vermouth-rojo/w/8062529

- **Retailer tasting testimonial: traditional, full-bodied, surprising, fresh, and aromatic.**
  - Evidence: La Despensa del Bierzo uses this tasting positioning and notes a soft initial
    sweetness with a small bitter finish.
  - Source: https://ladespensadelbierzo.es/producto/vermouth-forzudo-rojo/

## Notes for Product-Page Usage

- Strongest claims for UI badges:
  - Forzudo Rojo: "IWSC Silver Outstanding", "Guia Penin 92", "Brussels Double Gold".
  - Forzudo Blanco: "Brussels Gold", "Guia Penin 91", "London Spirits Bronze".
  - Tabira: "CINVE Silver 2019", "Vivino 4.4".
  - Sardino Rojo: "90 points, Nicks", "Vivino 3.8".
  - Carmeleta Orange: "4.9/5 from 49 PrivateCeller reviews".
  - Carmeleta Bianco: "Montserrat white vermouth category prize", "PrivateCeller 5/5".

- Safer wording:
  - Prefer "reported by", "listed by", or "rated on" for retailer/rating-platform claims.
  - Use formal competition names only where the source is a competition page, producer post,
    reputable press, or specialist directory.
  - Avoid implying the customer reviews are universal ratings; label them by platform.
